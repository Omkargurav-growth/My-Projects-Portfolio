### 2. **DSA Project: Red Sea Crisis Communication Analysis**
 **File**: (https://github.com/Omkargurav-growth/My-Projects-Portfolio/blob/9aa130b54a9e2ca0568df6c6bb5c029e3b6d0377/04-Project/SDG%20Project%20Report%20-%20NatureSync%20Alliance.pdf)  
**Original Document**: SDG-Project-Report_By_NatureSync-Alliance.docx  
**Submitted**: October 7, 2025

#### Key Details:
- **Title**: The Red Sea Crisis 2024 and Its Impact on Indian Automotive Industry
- **Focus**: Crisis Communication Analysis of Audi India
- **Crisis Period**: November 2023 - October 2024
- **Student**: Omkar Tanaji Gurav

#### Crisis Context:
- 190+ Houthi attacks on vessels in Red Sea
- Forced rerouting around Cape of Good Hope
- 10-14 day delay in shipments
- 30%+ increase in freight and insurance costs

#### Communication Strategies Analyzed:

**Before Crisis**
- Minimal proactive risk communication
- Over-reliance on established shipping routes

**During Crisis**
- Rapid public acknowledgment
- Transparent stakeholder communication
- Regular ETA updates
- Contextualized messaging

**After Crisis**
- Optimistic normalization messaging
- Local assembly plant exploration
- Multi-channel communication

#### Key Recommendations:
1. Dedicated stakeholder communication portals
2. Real-time shipment tracking dashboards
3. Emergency helplines and AI-powered support
4. Enhanced international customer relations
5. Media and regulatory collaboration protocols
