## Key Objectives
- Address SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation
- Focus on Doddakere Lake restoration in Anekal
- Implement community-led solutions with IoT monitoring
- Promote sustainable water management practices

## Project Solution: Alliance NeerSetu
A smart, eco-friendly lake rejuvenation system combining:
- Raspberry Pi-based IoT controller
- Water quality sensors (pH, TSS, Alkalinity)
- Phytoremediation beds for natural purification
- Real-time data monitoring via ThingsSpeak platform
- Community engagement and monitoring programs

## SDG Alignment
- **Primary**: SDG 6 - Clean Water and Sanitation
- **Secondary**: SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities), SDG 13 (Climate Action), SDG 15 (Life on Land), SDG 17 (Partnerships)
