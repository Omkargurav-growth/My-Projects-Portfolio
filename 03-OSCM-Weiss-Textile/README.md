### Key Solutions Implemented

#### 1. **Color-Coded Storage Bins**
- Visual management system for fabric types and accessories
- Reduces retrieval time and minimizes picking errors
- Aligned with 5S methodology principles

#### 2. **Dedicated Zones & Aisle Markings**
- Segregation of Raw Material (RM), Work-In-Progress (WIP), and Finished Goods (FG)
- Clear floor markings and zone identification
- Prevents material commingling and improves safety

#### 3. **FIFO Integration with SAP HANA**
- Real-time batch tracking and material aging monitoring
- Automated alerts for products approaching expiry
- Enhanced traceability and audit readiness
- Supports production planning with accurate material availability

#### 4. **Conveyor Belt Automation**
- Replaces manual material transfers (Cutting â†’ Packing)
- Reduces labor fatigue and speeds up production flow
- Supports consistent production rhythm
